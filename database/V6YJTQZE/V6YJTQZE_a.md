Indeed,

$$
\begin{aligned}
  \langle Au, v\rangle &= \sum_{i} \overline{(Au)_i} v_i \\\\
    &= \sum_{i} \sum_{j} \overline{a_{ij} u_j} v_i \\\\
    &= \sum_{i} \sum_{j} a_{ji} \overline{u_j} v_i \\\\
    &= \sum_{j} \overline{u_j} \sum_{i} a_{ji} v_i \\\\
    &= \sum_{j} \overline{u_j} (Av)_j = \langle u, A v \rangle.
\end{aligned}
$$
