The *matrix* of a linear map $L: V \to W$ of finite-dimensional vector spaces $V$ and $W$ over a field $k$ with respect to bases $\\{v_i\\}$ and $\\{w_j\\}$ is a matrix $A$ such that the diagram

$$
\begin{array}{ccc}
V & \xrightarrow{L} & W \\
\downarrow & & \downarrow \\
k^n & \xrightarrow{A} & k^m
\end{array}
$$

commute.
