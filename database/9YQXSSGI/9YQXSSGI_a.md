The *Killing form* on a Lie algebra $\mathfrak{g}$ over a field $k$ is a symmetric bilinear form $B: \mathfrak{g} \times \mathfrak{g} \to k$

$$
B(X, Y) := \mathop{\mathrm{tr}}(\mathrm{ad}_X \mathrm{ad}_Y)
$$

where $\mathrm{ad}_X : \mathfrak{g} \to \mathfrak{g}$ is given by $\mathrm{ad}_X(Y) := [X, Y]$.
